Towers of Hanoi:

The goal of the towers of hanoi is to move the stack of rings starting on peg 1 and move them to peg 3. However, only the top ring of a stack can be moved, and no ring may ever be placed on top of a smaller ring. If you would like to see how you can solve this puzzle optimally, watch the solver do it for you.
